Technology: Good Servant, Bad Master.

Technology plays a vital role in today’s world. From enhancing communication and managing time to advancing manufacturing and healthcare, technological tools significantly shape our daily lives. For students at UA Grantham, technology is especially important, allowing them to succeed academically regardless of their location. Of course, technology comes with trade-offs, and at times it can feel like it dominates our lives. Let’s explore the positive, negative, and potentially harmful aspects of technology and consider how we can use it effectively without falling into common pitfalls.
